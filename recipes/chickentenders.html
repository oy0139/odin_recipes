Chicken Tenders Recipe

Description

Chicken tenders when done right are easily one of the most delicious foods that you can possibly make. As a kid I enjoyed going to the Bronx Zoo not only because of my love for animals, but also because of their AMAZING CHICKEN TENDERS!! However, the prices have grown significantly, which has prompted me to try and experiment with ways to make my homeade chicken recipes taste as good as those at the Zoo. Turns out, I have found a way to make them even better! They are crunchy but juicy on the inside, and did I mention how ridiculously easy they are to make?! You won't ever want to eat chicken tenders any other way after trying this recipe, and thats a promise

Ingredients

  • 30 saltine crackers
  • 2 tablespoons dry potato flakes
  • 1 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into 1 inch strips
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil, or as needed
